# ICT Strategy Specification

## Overview
The ICT (Inner Circle Trader) strategy is a price action methodology focused on institutional order flow patterns.

## Key Concepts
- **Liquidity**: Buy-side and sell-side liquidity pools
- **Order Blocks**: Institutional order zones
- **Fair Value Gaps (FVG)**: Imbalances in price
- **Market Structure Shifts**: Trend changes
- **Optimal Trade Entry (OTE)**: Pullback entry zones

## Session Times
- **London Kill Zone**: 2:00-5:00 AM EST
- **New York Kill Zone**: 8:30-11:00 AM EST
- **London Close**: 10:00-12:00 PM EST

## Entry Criteria
1. Identify liquidity sweep
2. Wait for market structure shift
3. Enter on return to order block or FVG
4. Target opposing liquidity pool

## Risk Management
- Stop loss: Below/above entry order block
- Take profit: 2:1 or 3:1 RR minimum
- Position size: 1-2% risk per trade

## Timeframes
- Higher timeframe bias: 4H / 1H
- Entry timeframe: 15M / 5M
- Confirmation: 1M
